Fried Chicken

Description

This oven-fried chicken recipe is made with simple ingredients you probably already have in hand.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degree F.
  2. Place flour in a shallow plate and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Dredge chicken piece by piece in the flour, then the egg, then the bread crumbs.
  4. Pour oil into the large baking dish.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes.
