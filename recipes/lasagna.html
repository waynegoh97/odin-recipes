Lasagna

Description

It is composed of traditional Mexican ingredients layered over the top of one another.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Lasagna noodle

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
